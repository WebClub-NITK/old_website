We are the NITK Web Club.

We are a group of motivated, passionate students who provide help, technical support, education (in the form of talks, lectures, workshops, demonstrations, code showcases, etc), fun (in the form of contests, events, discussions, brainstorming sessions, etc) to each other as well as to anyone else who is interested; other students, faculty, and any other denizen of NITK (National Institute of Technology Karnataka) Surathkal.

We love code.

FOSS Wins

Web Club NITK is the primary student association at NITK Surathkal that promotes the FSF and FOSS philosophy. We love what we do, and we learn a lot from it too.
