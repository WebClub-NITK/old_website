DHCP Relay Enhancement for ns-3

  • Project: Enabling Support for relays in DHCP as an enhancement to ns-3
  • Students: GVS Tushaar, Ujjwal Pasupulety
  • Mentor: Ankit Deepak
  • Abstract: Improving on our Mentor's implementation of DHCP in ns-3, the aim is to add the enhancement of including relays in a given network. While the basic form of DHCP works with only a client-server model, in the real world, there are relays between the two in large networks that act as mediators between the client and server in assigning new IP addresses and relinquishing unused ones back into the address pool. Our goal is to implement a real world simulation of DHCP in ns-3
